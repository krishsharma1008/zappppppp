## Overview

## Earning XP

### Module Completion

The primary way to earn XP is by completing course modules. XP rewards are based on module difficulty:

| Difficulty | XP Reward | Description |
|------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Warmup** | 75 XP | Introductory modules, foundational concepts |
| **Core** | 125 XP | Standard modules, main learning content |
| **Challenge** | 175 XP | Advanced modules, complex problem-solving |

**Important Notes:**
- XP is awarded **only on first completion** of a module
- Re-attempting a module does not award additional XP
- All tests must pass to receive XP

### Daily Streaks

Maintain your learning momentum by claiming your daily streak:

- **Base Reward**: 120 XP
- **Streak Bonus**: +8 XP per consecutive day

**Streak Rules:**
- Claim your streak once per day
- Streaks reset if you miss a day
- Claim before midnight to maintain your streak
- Streaks continue across course completions

### Course Completion

Earn bonus XP for completing entire courses:
- **Beginner Courses**: 500 XP bonus
- **Intermediate Courses**: 1,000 XP bonus
- **Advanced Courses**: 1,500 XP bonus

## Daily Streaks

### How Streaks Work

1. **Claim Daily**: Visit the Daily Streak page and click "Claim Streak"
2. **Earn XP**: Receive base XP + bonus based on your current streak
3. **Maintain Momentum**: Come back tomorrow to continue your streak
4. **Earn Badges**: Reach milestone streaks to earn special badges

### Streak Calculation

Your streak increases by 1 each day you claim it:
- If you claimed yesterday: Streak continues (+1 day)
- If you missed yesterday: Streak resets to 1
- If you already claimed today: Come back tomorrow

### Streak Rewards

## Progress Tracking

### Dashboard

Your dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your learning progress:

- **Total XP**: Your lifetime XP total
- **Current Tier**: Your current tier and progress to next tier
- **Streak**: Your current and longest streaks
- **Solved Today**: Modules completed today
- **Review Queue**: Modules flagged for review
- **Course Progress**: Completion percentage and XP earned per course

### Course Pages

Each course page shows:
- **Course Completion**: Percentage of modules completed
- **Earned XP**: XP earned from this course
- **Total XP**: Maximum XP available in this course
- **Module Status**: Which modules are completed, in progress, or locked
- **Module XP**: XP value for each module

### XP Transaction History

View your complete XP history:
- **Source**: Where the XP came from (module, streak, bonus, etc.)
- **Amount**: XP gained or lost
- **Date**: When the transaction occurred
- **Notes**: Additional context about the transaction

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I lose XP?

No, XP is never deducted. However, admin adjustments may occur in rare cases of system errors.

### What happens if I miss a day in my streak?

Your streak resets to 0, and you'll start fresh the next time you claim. Your longest streak record is preserved.

### Can I earn XP from the same module multiple times?

No, XP is awarded only on first completion. However, you can re-attempt modules for practice without earning additional XP.

### How often is the leaderboard updated?

The leaderboard updates in real-time as students earn XP. Rankings may take a few seconds to reflect recent changes.

### What if I reach Grandmaster tier?

Congratulations! You'll remain at Grandmaster tier and continue earning XP. Your total XP will continue to grow, and you'll be recognized as one of the top students.

### Can I see other students' profiles?

Currently, you can see other students' names, tiers, and XP on the leaderboard. Detailed profile viewing is coming soon.

### Are there any rewards for high XP?

Beyond tier badges and leaderboard recognition, we're planning to introduce additional rewards such as:
- Course discounts
- Early access to new content
- Exclusive community events
- Certificates of achievement

## Tips for Maximizing XP

1. **Prioritize Challenge Modules**: They offer the highest XP rewards (175 XP)
2. **Never Miss a Day**: Daily streaks compound quickly and can earn you thousands of XP over time
3. **Complete Full Courses**: Earn bonus XP for course completion
4. **Stay Consistent**: Regular, daily learning is more rewarding than sporadic bursts
5. **Set Goals**: Aim for the next tier or a specific XP milestone
6. **Compete Friendly**: Use the leaderboard for motivation, not stress
7. **Focus on Learning**: XP is a byproduct of genuine learning and skill development
